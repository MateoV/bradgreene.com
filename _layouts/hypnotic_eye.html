Brad Greene writes and illustrates books, comics, and graphic novels. Currently drawing a new comic titled BLACK EYE and releasing issues on this site.

The work has been called "dark", "somber", "experimental" and "edgy." Nervous lines and stretches of black ink create a surreal minimalist approach to horror and the unknown.

He is also a home recording musician constantly releasing free ambient music under the name SKELETON.
